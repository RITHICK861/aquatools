Why Track Aquarium pH?

A stable pH is far more important than a "perfect" pH. Rapid pH swings cause severe osmotic shock to fish.

Use this manual pH tracker to log your values over time so you can spot if a substrate or decore piece is leaching carbonates, or if old tank syndrome is causing a pH crash.
