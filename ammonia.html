What Causes Ammonia Spikes?

Ammonia is incredibly toxic to fish and invertebrates. It often spikes due to overfeeding, dead matter left in the tank, or cleaning your filter media in tap water (which kills the beneficial bacteria).

Using this Ammonia guide ensures that you take the proper steps (like Prime dosing and water changes) before your fish suffer from gill burns or suffocation.
