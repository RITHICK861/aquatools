Can They Live Together?

This basic Fish Compatibility Checker helps new hobbyists avoid catastrophic mixing errors at the fish store. Never mix aggressive territorial cichlids with small, peaceful community fish.

Always research individual species needs! Even within "community" fish, some require drastically different temperatures or pH levels. For example, African Cichlids require very hard, alkaline water while South American Tetras require soft, acidic water.
