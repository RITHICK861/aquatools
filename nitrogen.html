Patience is Key in the Nitrogen Cycle

The aquarium nitrogen cycle is the process of establishing beneficial bacteria that convert lethal Ammonia into Nitrite, and then into less harmful Nitrate.

Using this Nitrogen Cycle Tracker helps beginners avoid "New Tank Syndrome." Always dose an ammonia source (fish food or pure liquid ammonia) to start the process. A fully cycled tank will process 2ppm ammonia completely to zero within 24 hours.
