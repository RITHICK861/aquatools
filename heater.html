How Strong Should Your Aquarium Heater Be?

Using this heater wattage calculator helps prevent under-heating or catastrophic over-heating. As a rule of thumb, you need 1.5W per liter for small temperature differences, and up to 2.5W per liter for colder rooms.

Pro tip: For larger tanks (200L+), consider splitting the recommended wattage between two smaller heaters. If one fails in the 'on' position, it will take much longer to overheat the tank!
