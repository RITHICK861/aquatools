Don't Overstock Your Tank

This fish tank stocking calculator uses a conservative 1cm of adult fish per liter of water as a baseline (this varies by filtration and fish type/girth).

Overstocking leads to high ammonia, stressed fish, and excessive maintenance. When calculating, always use the adult length of the fish, not their current juvenile size!
