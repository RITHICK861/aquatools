Don't Buy "Tank Busters"

Pet stores often sell adorable, tiny fish that will quickly outgrow a standard aquarium. The Fish Growth Estimator highlights the most notorious tank busters.

For example, Common Plecos are sold at 5cm but can easily reach 60cm long! Always know the adult size before purchasing. Choosing alternatives like the Bristlenose Pleco can save you from needing a 100+ gallon upgrade later.
