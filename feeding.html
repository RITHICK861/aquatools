Don't Overfeed Your Fish!

The #1 cause of aquarium crashes is overfeeding. Fish need much less food than you think. Using this Feeding Schedule Generator will help you provide variety without polluting the water.

Always incorporate at least one "fasting day" per week to allow the fishes' digestive systems to clear out, preventing swim bladder disease.
